Smart Drone Delivery -by : Ojasva Mishra

This report examines smart drone delivery systems as an emerging technology in logistics. Drones offer faster delivery times, reduced environmental impact, and access to remote areas, though regulatory and technical challenges remain.

The global drone delivery market is projected to reach $5.6 billion by 2030. Advancements in battery technology, AI, and regulatory frameworks will accelerate adoption, initially focusing on medical deliveries, rural areas, and time-sensitive packages.

Blockchain -by : Yuvraj Singh

Blockchain is a decentralized, immutable digital ledger technology that securely records and links transaction data in blocks across a distributed network, ensuring transparency and resistance to tampering

The global blockchain market is projected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of around 58.3% from 2025 to 2030, reaching an estimated value of $306 billion by 2030, with hybrid blockchain solutions accounting for 42% of the market share

Robotics -by : Vaibhav Pandey

Robotics is reshaping modern healthcare by enabling precise, minimally invasive surgeries and streamlining hospital operations. Robotic-assisted systems aid surgeons with enhanced control and accuracy, reducing recovery time for patients. In rehabilitation and elder care, robots are offering consistent support and monitoring. These innovations are driving better outcomes and improving overall patient experience.

Robotics in healthcare is projected to reach a market value of over $43 billion by 2028, driven by demand for precision and automation. Studies show robotic-assisted surgeries reduce hospital stays by 21% and lower complication rates by 30%. From surgical robots like da Vinci to autonomous delivery units, robotics is boosting efficiency, safety, and patient outcomes across the healthcare system.

Vaibhav Pandey

Product Manager at Apple

California, U.S
